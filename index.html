Seed Bloom Charity

Who We Are

Seed Bloom Charity is dedicated to supporting Jewish life in Voronezh. We help nurture hidden Jewish roots—especially those buried under generations of silence and erasure—like seeds never watered. We provide warmth, guidance, and nourishment so they can grow, thrive, and proudly blossom. We strive to make a lasting impact through education, cultural programs, and direct support for families and individuals in need.

Our Mission

We believe that every act of kindness can grow into something bigger. Our mission is to nurture and strengthen Jewish life by providing resources, opportunities, and a sense of belonging for everyone.
