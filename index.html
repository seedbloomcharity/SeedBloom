Who We Are

Seed Bloom Charity is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) dedicated to supporting Jewish life in places where Jewish identity was suppressed, and where an entire generation never had the chance to know what it truly means to be Jewish.

We help nurture hidden Jewish roots — buried under decades of silence and erasure — like seeds finally receiving the water they have always needed.

Our Mission

We believe that every act of kindness can grow into something bigger. Our mission is to nurture and strengthen Jewish life by providing resources, opportunities, and a sense of belonging for everyone.
